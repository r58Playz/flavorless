FAQ

Who is eligible?

Flavortown is for highschoolers! You need to be 13-18 years old to participate.

How much does it cost?

100% free* - all the prizes are donated to us or paid for by us! *customs might occur outside of the US

What types of projects count?

All kinds of technical projects as long as it's open-source!

How many projects can I build?

There's no limit! Cook as much as you can!

Is this legit?

Yup! This summer we ran Summer of Making , and before that we ran High Seas —both programs gave out similar prizes for building personal projects.

A project by Hack Club

Hack Club is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit and network of 60k+ technical high schoolers. We believe you learn best by building so we're creating community and providing grants so you can make awesome projects. In the past few years, we've partnered with GitHub to run Summer of Making, hosted the world's longest hackathon on land, and ran Canada's largest high school hackathon.

At Hack Club, students aren't just learning, they're shipping.
